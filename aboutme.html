About Me

A little bit of info about myself and what I do.

Who Am I?

My name is Zgell, and I've always been interested in computers and electronics. I'm a computer programmer, an electronics hobbyist and an engineering student. Throughout my whole life I've always had a lot of fun with designing things, whether they were computer programs to automate tasks, or crazy ideas for fun electronic devices. Over the years I've taken the time to learn and acquire skills pertaining to these areas of interest so that I can work on some of these projects.

Some of my interests that inspire my projects are:

  • Computer Programming
  • Computer Hardware
  • Mathematics
  • Rocketry and Technology used for Space Exploration
  • Electronics and Circuit Design
  • Robotics
  • Video Game Design
  • Hiking in the Mountains

I've created this website that you're viewing right now for a couple of reasons. Firstly, I wanted a centralized place to keep documentation for all of my projects. I wanted to keep everything related to my projects online and public so that anyone could try to recreate my projects or build off of my code. Secondly, I want to use this website as a portfolio to showcase all of my work. My goal is to not only showcase what I've worked on, but every bit of detail related to my work that onlookers may be interested in. This website gives me a platform to document and explain various projects, the story behind how they were made, and how others can recreate or use them.
